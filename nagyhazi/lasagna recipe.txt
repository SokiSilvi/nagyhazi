   |                                               1. Melt the butter                              |
   | Prepare the meat-sauce:                       2. Pour the flour (Do not burn it!)             |
   |  1. Fry the chopped veggies in olive oil      3. Upon constant stir add the milk              |
   |  2. Add the minced meat                       4. Boil for 5 minutes then spice it             |  
   |  3. When fried, add the garlic and spices                                                     |
   |  4. Pour the wine, boil for 5 minutes        Assemble:                                        |
   |  5. Pour the passata, the milk and the water  1. Make 3 layers: 3 sheets, meat, sauce         |
   |  6. Boil for 45 minutes in medium heat        2. Pour the remaining sauce and parmesan        |
   |  7. Pour 150 ml of the ready bechamel         3. Bake for 20-25 mins 200 °C, grill for 5 mins |
